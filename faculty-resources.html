01 · Information processing

Past files, curriculum research,
policy judgement, assignment reviews.

For when you have a folder full of PDFs and a question — and you want the answer in two minutes, not two hours.

Claude Projects
claude.ai · Anthropic

Drop a semester's worth of files into one space — every chat after has the full context, no re-uploading.

claude.ai/projects↗
ChatGPT Folders
chatgpt.com · OpenAI

Group chats by class, batch, or research topic so threads don't disappear into the timeline.

chatgpt.com↗
NotebookLM Bonus
notebooklm.google.com · Google

Upload PDFs and lecture notes — get a podcast-style audio summary you can listen to on the commute.

Two AI hosts discuss your material like a radio show.

Lecture decks, grade sheets,
reports the dean asked for yesterday.

Type a brief, get a polished deck in minutes. Beats fighting PowerPoint themes for a routine lecture.
